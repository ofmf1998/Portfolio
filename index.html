Audiovisual and Advertising Communication student at Unitec, Honduras. Passionate about filmmaking, 20th century cinema, photography and Argentine rock. After an exchange program to Italy in 2015, he discovered his love for movies and embarked on his journey as a director and photographer. Back home, Mejia faced the reality that filmmaking and cinema are not as easily accessible as in Europe. Having to experience two different career paths, medicine and engineering, he then decided to face his desire to be involved in a creative setting and career. Wishing to see Honduras' filmmaking scene grow, Mejia has been honing his skills and collaborating on short films with local talents.
